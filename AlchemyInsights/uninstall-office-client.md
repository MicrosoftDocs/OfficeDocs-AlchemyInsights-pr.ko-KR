> PC: [PC에 대 한 Office를 제거 합니다.](https://support.office.com/article/Uninstall-Office-from-a-PC-9dd49b83-264a-477a-8fcc-2fdf5dbf61d8.aspx)
    
> Mac: [Mac 용 Office 2016 제거](https://support.office.com/article/Uninstall-Office-2016-for-Mac-eefa1199-5b58-43af-8a3d-b73dc1a8cae3.aspx) 또는 [Mac 용 Office 2011을 제거 합니다.](https://support.office.com/article/Uninstall-Office-2011-for-Mac-4bfcd230-0ea1-4656-bf30-dbfa44d358fa.aspx)
